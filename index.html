Aerosmith Bio

Band Members

Aerosmith is founded in Boston Massachusets and it also includes band Members:

  • Steven Tyler - Vocals
  • Joe Perry - Guitar
  • Tom Hamilton - Bass
  • Brad Whitford - Guitar
  • Joey Kramer - Drums

Discography

Aerosmith has released numerous albums over the years, including:

  • Aerosmith (1973)
  • Toys in the Attic (1975)
  • Rocks (1976)
